What is Soul Bound?

Soul Bound is a character driven tactical role playing game that throws players into the role of a new member of a guild of mages. The player and the guild are being drawn into the events surrounding the awakening of a long forgotten curse on the world. Travel the continent, discover new allies and enemies, and forge bonds strong enough to overcome a danger that has come to turn the world against itself.
