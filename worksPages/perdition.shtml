Perdition


The Project

Perdition is a table-top board game prototype. It is a fast paced, dungeon-crawling, rogue-like adventure game where players make the best of the abilities and items they receive to defeat 7 levels of monsters and bosses.



My Responsibilities - Project Lead / Designer

  • Headed team of 4 others to create physical prototype of game content.
  • Developed base game proposal for class, and was 1 of 6 proposals chosen of 30.
  • Established base rules and mechanics to game, including combat interaction, loot system, and abilities system.
  • Maintained schedule for production and creation of prototype.
