What is HEXES?

HEXES!! is a fast-paced party card game where you hex your friends with rules they must follow! Your goal is to stay in the game while managing to follow the rules on all the hexes you happen to receive. If you break one of these rules, youâ€™re out! On paper, it might sound pretty simple, but in practice, it can be incredibly difficult. The hex rules combine with each other to create some absolutely hilarious moments. Fun to play with friends and family!

My Role - Team Lead

Of our team of 5, I was Team Lead. I worked closely with Norman Greenberg, the Lead Designer. Along with a few others, we put together the Indiegogo campaign, including reaching out to printers for quotes, study of other successful crowdfunding campaigns, and managing communications with Hasbro and other entities.

We managed the social media and weekly / milestone updates for the Indiegogo campaign.
