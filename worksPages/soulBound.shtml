Soul Bound


The Project

Built using Unity 4 3D.

Soul Bound is a RPG that utilizes a turn-based combat system, character driven story, and item and skill customization. It is currently being developed by a team of 11 people. All assets are being created in-house, including art and modelling, sound and music design, and programming.



My Responsibilities - Producer

  • Manage and organize team of 11 people to create a game in 16 weeks.
  • Established and maintained development schedule.
  • Identified crucial development items key to completion of project on time.
  • Oversight of creative and technical development.
  • Ensured timely delivery of milestones through use of Microsoft Project and Excel.
  • Pitch game idea to MAGIC Center at RIT for continued development over Summer 2015 (Unpaid Internship). This will take place April time-frame.
