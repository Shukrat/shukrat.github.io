What is Bloxeo?

Bloxeo is a group brainstorming webapp. Allows teams of up to 15 people to piece words or phrases together into collections, cote, and settle on one idea to go forward with as a team.

My Role - Project Lead

  • Worked with team leads to define tasks, sprint requirements and manage bug reports and triage.
  • Provided and maintained crucial communication between teams
